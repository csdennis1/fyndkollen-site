Questions

How do I activate after buying?

You'll get an FS-XXXX-XXXX key by email seconds after payment. Open the Fyndkollen extension, paste it into the "Fyndkollen Pro" box, and click Activate.

How do credits work?

One credit = one appraisal. If a listing was recently appraised by anyone, you get the cached result for free — no credit spent. Credits never expire.

Do credits stack if I buy again?

Yes. Buy another pack with the same email and the credits add onto your existing key automatically — no new key to manage.

Do I need my own AI key?

No. Credits run through our hosted backend. (You can still plug in your own Anthropic key if you prefer — that bypasses credits entirely.)

Refunds?

The free tier lets you try before you buy. If a pack doesn't work for you, reply to your receipt within 14 days and we'll refund unused credits.
